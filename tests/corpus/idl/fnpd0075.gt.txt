BACKGROUND AND PURPOSE
Following the original June 9th introduction in Denver and
New Orleans, ..T. Brown was expanded into fields 4 and 9.
Advertising and "down the street" activity began September 15.
This topline report is based upon data which was collected
eight weeks after the start of advertising.
The purpose of this research is to measure levels of
awareness, trial, purchase and attitudes toward L.T. Brown
in the expansion areas.
METHODOLOGY
A total of 602 random telephone interviews were conducted
via WATS lines from a facility outside of Philadelphia, Pa.
These interviews were among smokers located within fields
4
and 9. The sample was structured so that one half would
represent field 4 and one half would represent field 9.
Interviewing was confined to SMSA's within these fields and
!
the number of interviews were skewed proportionately to the
population of the SMSA's. In- addition, the sample was equally
divided between men and women aged 18-55 who smoke ten or more
cigarettes per day. Interviewing took place between November
11 and November 25, 1975.

SUMMARY
In fields 4 and 9 L.T. Brown achieves awareness levels similar
to Wave II of Denver and New Orleans but directionally ahead of the
Wave I performance in these two original test markets. Between
fields 4 and 9, key measure scores are similar with the exception
of advertising awareness and purchase, where a slight advantage
goes to field 4.
Compared to More, L.T. Brown is clearly behind on these aware-
ness, trial and purchase measures. The levels achieved on these
measures are similar to those achieved by Saratoga with the exception
of brand awareness and purchase (particularly in field 9) where
Saratoga has the advantage.
Among 18-24 year old males and females awareness, trial and
purchase of L.T. Brown are at lower levels than observed in Denver
and New Orleans after 8 weeks out. Among 25-29 year old women
these measures have increased very noticibly, especially advertising
awareness and trial. Between men 30 and over in Denver and New
Orleans (Wave I) and fields 4 and 9 not a great deal difference is
noticed -- awareness is up while trial is slightly down. Women
in this age group registered upward movement on their measures.
